Real Good Soup Recipe

This soup is hands down the best you will ever eat. If you don't like it, you are missing a tongue. Not only is it dam delicious, it's also the easiest soup that you will ever make. Follow these simple instructions and bathe your senses in all its glory:

Ingredients:

  • Soup
  • tobasco sauce
  • new khaki pants

Steps:

  1. Go to the supermarket and buy soup
  2. Ride home on your bicycle according to road safety protocols
  3. Get home and check social media on the couch
  4. Walk into your utility room and forget when you went in there
  5. Throw your tinned soup into the microwave on high for 10 minutes
  6. Burn your tongue on the bit off the edge that you sampled
  7. Put on your favourite Youtube channel and douse your soup in tobasco sauce
  8. Enjoy it but not witout spilling red sauce on your new khaki pants
